What Are You Looking For In Your Future Residence?

Consider whether you’re open to renting first, the amenities you want (pool, gym, pet-friendly, etc.), whether you want a new build or existing home, proximity to downtown, transit options, local school quality, your food or nightlife interests, and more.

• Are you OK with renting prior to buying a home?
• Interested in a new build or a pre-existing home?
• Proximity to work or downtown?
• Public transit or prefer to drive?
• School systems needed?
• Food scene, nightlife, or quieter neighborhoods?
• Shopping preferences, or dining style?
• Anything else that reflects your personal tastes and lifestyle?

And please feel free to prioritize your list of needs so our AI can fine-tune your creative journey even more.

What Kind of Help Will You Need?

Please describe what you’ll need in order to make this next amazing step possible.

• Do you have to break a lease? If so, how much might that cost?
• How many people (and pets!) are part of your move?
• Will you be driving or flying (or both)?
• Do you have parents or extended family coming along?
• Basically, anything that could add to your total moving costs.
• Or, if you just have a number in mind that would make you feel whole and taken care of, you can share that too.

Just a quick note about our relocation bonuses: You do have to start at RDC before receiving your assistance (standard procedure at most companies). But you’ll receive it in your first check, so you’re not out of pocket for too long—maybe just a credit card billing cycle, if that.

Why Are You Interested in Moving? (The More Personal, The Better!)

Here are some starter questions:

• Is this where you grew up or went to school?
• Do you have a good community of family/friends there?
• Are you looking to improve quality of life (more home, better schools, healthcare, lower taxes)?
• Is this about retirement or post-career lifestyle?

The more you share, the clearer your vision becomes—for you and for us. By helping us understand your motives, you're painting your own future success. Pumped? We sure are!
